## The Core Insight

> "The real unlock for product managers isn't in simply using AI to answer your questions. But it's instead in building AI-powered systems and workflows that actually automate the work we do every day."

Claude Code is an **agentic platform**, not a chatbot. The distinction matters: it executes autonomous, multi-step tasks rather than responding to individual queries.

## Five High-Value PM Workflows

### 1. Strategy Critique
Store strategic frameworks (positioning, value proposition, TAM analysis) as local markdown knowledge files. Claude loads this context and systematically evaluates your product strategy across dimensions like target audience fit, value proposition clarity, and competitive differentiation — replacing ad hoc review with a repeatable, comprehensive audit.

### 2. Competitor Pricing Updates
Use browser automation to visit competitor pricing pages in real time, extract current data, and generate strategic commentary. Solves the core problem with traditional AI research: staleness. You get live data, not training-data snapshots.

### 3. Customer Interview Summarization
Three-step pipeline:
1. Transcribe raw Zoom recordings using Whisper
2. Summarize each interview against a prescriptive template capturing problems, workflows, and verbatim quotes
3. Synthesize across interviews to surface prevalent pain points with supporting evidence

Produces consistent, comparable notes at scale — impossible manually across dozens of interviews.

### 4. Data Question Answering
Converts natural language questions into SQL, executes against your database, and returns formatted HTML reports with visualizations. Eliminates the bottleneck of routing questions through a data team and waiting days for results.

### 5. Release Notes Generation
Inspects GitHub commits and changed files, then produces user-facing release notes matched to your product's established voice and tone — trained from previous release note examples. Consistent quality without the blank-page problem.

## Coverage Across PM Domains

These five workflows span the full PM remit:

- **Vision** — strategy critique
- **Strategy** — competitive pricing intelligence
- **Design** — customer insight synthesis
- **Execution** — data Q&A, release notes

The common thread: automate repetitive, structured tasks where consistency and comprehensiveness matter more than creativity.
